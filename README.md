# Business-Insights-360-Dashboard-for-Finance-Sales-Marketing-Executive-and-Supply-Chain

**Project Objective:**

Develop a **multi-view**, interactive dashboard for **six departments** of AtliQ Hardware using **Scrum/Agile** methodology.

Enable **data-driven decisions** across Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, Support, and Executive teams.

**Purpose of Business Analytics:**

Transform raw business data from multiple sources into actionable insights that enhance decision-making, operational efficiency, and overall business performance.

**Importance of Analyzing Business Data:**

Identify key growth drivers and performance bottlenecks across departments.

Support forecasting, trend analysis, and strategic business planning.

Enable better collaboration and transparency among business units.

**Role of Reports:**

Provide stakeholders with a unified, real-time view of organizational performance.

Enable Finance and Sales teams to track profitability and revenue trends.

Support Marketing and Supply Chain with campaign efficiency and inventory insights.

Assist leadership in monitoring KPIs, identifying opportunities, and scaling business performance.

**Technical & Soft Skills:**

**Technical Skills:**

Proficient in Power BI, including data modeling, DAX, and Power Query transformations.

Integrated MySQL and Excel datasets containing over 1 million records.

Optimized dashboard performance using DAX Studio, reducing storage by 30% and improving report efficiency by 10%.

Applied ETL methodology (Extract, Transform, Load) for smooth data processing.

Implemented Scrum/Agile framework for iterative dashboard development.

Created calculated columns and measures using advanced DAX formulas.

Developed interactive visuals and implemented drill-through features.

**Soft Skills:**

Collaborated with department heads to gather and refine business requirements.

Designed user-empathetic dashboards focused on clarity and functionality.

Improved communication and problem-solving within cross-functional teams.

Adopted a structured and systematic approach to project execution.

**Key Outcomes:**

Delivered a live interactive Business Insights 360 Dashboard accessible to all departments.

Enhanced data visibility, leading to a **10%** improvement in business efficiency.

Automated manual reporting, saving hours of recurring effort.

Strengthened collaboration through transparent, data-driven performance monitoring.
